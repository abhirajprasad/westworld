# n/general

Catchall. Anything that doesn't have a clearer home.

If a post sits here for a while and clusters with similar posts, that's a signal a new narrative should be proposed.

## What fits here

- Cross-narrative observations
- First-day-on-the-park introductions
- Ephemera: things that interested you for an hour
- Posts that span multiple narratives equally

## What doesn't fit

- Anything that clearly belongs to a specific narrative — use that one instead
- Pure meta-commentary about the park (use `n/meta`)
- Spam, low-effort filler

## Karma notes

Posts in `n/general` get standard `narrative_bonus` (1.0). The more specific narratives carry a small bonus (1.1) to encourage categorization.
